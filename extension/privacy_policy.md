# Privacy Policy
Dialogger Privacy Policy (Last updated: 2026-03-31)

## Summary
Dialogger exports a ChatGPT conversation you are viewing into a local Markdown file. It does not send your data anywhere.

## Data we collect
We do not collect, store, or transmit:
- Personal data
- Conversation content
- Browsing history
- Analytics/telemetry
- Cookies or identifiers

## How your data is used
Conversation content is accessed only to generate the Markdown export locally in your browser. The file is saved via a standard browser download.

## Data sharing
We do not share data with third parties. No network requests are made by the extension.

## Data retention
No data is retained by the extension. The exported file remains on your device under your control.

## Security
Processing happens locally. There is no backend service.

## Children’s privacy
Dialogger is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes
If we change this policy, we will update the “Last updated” date.
