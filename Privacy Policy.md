## Short description
Export ChatGPT conversations to a Markdown file with one click.

## Detailed description
Dialogger is a lightweight Chrome extension that exports the currently opened ChatGPT conversation into a local Markdown (.md) file.

## How it works
1. Open a ChatGPT conversation on chatgpt.com (or chat.openai.com)
2. Click the Dialogger extension icon
3. A Markdown file (chatgpt_export_YYYY-MM-DD-HH-MM-SS.md) is downloaded to your Downloads folder

## Privacy
- No accounts, no analytics, no trackers
- No data is sent to any server
- The export is generated locally in your browser

## Permissions
Runs only on chatgpt.com/* and chat.openai.com/* to read the conversation content and generate the export.

## Privacy Policy
Dialogger Privacy Policy (Last updated: 2026-03-31)

### Summary
Dialogger exports a ChatGPT conversation you are viewing into a local Markdown file. It does not send your data anywhere.

### Data we collect
We do not collect, store, or transmit:
- Personal data
- Conversation content
- Browsing history
- Analytics/telemetry
- Cookies or identifiers

### How your data is used
Conversation content is accessed only to generate the Markdown export locally in your browser. The file is saved via a standard browser download.

### Data sharing
We do not share data with third parties. No network requests are made by the extension.

### Data retention
No data is retained by the extension. The exported file remains on your device under your control.

### Security
Processing happens locally. There is no backend service.

### Children’s privacy
Dialogger is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect data from children.
